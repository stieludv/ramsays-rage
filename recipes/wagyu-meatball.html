Wagyu Meatball

Description

Oyyyy, be careful... Grodon will not be happy when he sees what you've done to the wagyu beef.

Classics never die - make the best meatball in the universe and serve it with tomato sauce. This is sure to make your guests absolutely impressed when you tell them it was made with wagyu beef.

Ingredients

  • Wagyu beef
  • Onion
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh parsley
  • Sea salt
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Mince the meat
  2. Mix the finely chopped onion into it
  3. Add the fresh basil, parsley and sea salt
  4. Add the pepper and olive oil
  5. Make balls (roll the meat mix into balls)
  6. Cook balls with tomato sauce (add at the end)
  7. Eat balls covered in tomato sauce
