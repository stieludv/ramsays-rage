Hawaii Pizza

Description

Gordon is sure to throw up when you show him this.

A beautiful and tasty hawaii pizza topped with curry, apes favourite berry (banans), ham and most importantly pineapple!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ham
  • Banana
  • Pineapple
  • Curry

Instructions

  1. Make your pizza dough into a circle with edges
  2. Smear tomato sacue on it
  3. Chop your ham into small pices and let them rain down evenly on the pizza
  4. Randomly chuck banana slices ontop of it
  5. Carefully place your pineapple bits in a nice pattern
  6. Top the thing with curry
  7. Slide it into the oven on a pizza stone
